Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

What this app does

SSAM Audit Workflow is an internal automation tool that reads emails from a designated Gmail account to process audit-related documents (P&L, Balance Sheet). It downloads attachments for analysis and marks processed emails as read.

Data collected

This app accesses Gmail message metadata (sender, subject line) and attachments from emails matching a specific subject pattern. No data is shared with third parties.

Data storage

Downloaded attachments and email metadata are stored locally on the user's computer. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond Google's own APIs.

Data retention

Processed email IDs are stored locally to prevent duplicate processing. Users can delete this data at any time.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the app administrator.
